How to use the simulator

1. Add components: Use the toolbar buttons to add Sources, Tanks, Sinks, and Controllers (PID) to the workspace.

2. Connect Pipes: Hold Ctrl and click a component to reveal its connection ports. Click a port to start a pipe, then click a port on another component to connect them.

3. Connect Controller (PID):
- Process Value (PV): Connect a Tank port to a PID port exactly like drawing a pipe.
- Control Valve (CV): Hold Ctrl and click a PID. All available control valves (red flow circles) will light up with a yellow glow. Click a flow circle to connect the PID output signal.

4. Advanced Routing: Drag the red flow circle to route the pipe freely. Hover and drag the invisible vertical/horizontal pipe segments to adjust 90-degree corners. Drag the red/green pipe ends to snap them to different component ports.

5. Multi-Select & Controls: Click and drag on the empty background to select and move/delete multiple items together. Double-click tanks, PIDs, or flow circles to edit their settings. Use Reset Levels to quickly set all tanks to 50%.

6. Error States: If a tank runs empty (0%) or overflows (100%), the simulation halts automatically to prevent errors and displays a large red X.
